Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 22, 2025

Overview

Odoo Integration for Gmail ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

Developer: OdooIntegrations Team

Information We Collect

Data Accessed from Gmail

The Extension accesses the following information from Gmail when you explicitly open an email:

  • Sender and recipient email addresses: Used to search for matching contacts in your Odoo instance
  • Email subject and body: Used when you choose to create opportunities, tasks, or tickets in Odoo
  • Email date and metadata: Used for logging emails to Odoo records

Data Accessed from Odoo

The Extension retrieves the following information from your Odoo instance:

  • Contact information (names, emails, phone numbers, companies)
  • CRM opportunities associated with contacts
  • Project tasks associated with contacts
  • Helpdesk tickets associated with contacts
  • Company information and related contacts

Configuration Data

The Extension stores your Odoo instance configuration locally:

  • Odoo instance URL
  • Database name
  • Authentication credentials
  • User preferences (visible sections settings)

How We Use Your Information

The Extension uses your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Display Contact Information: Show relevant Odoo contact details in Gmail's sidebar
  • Create Odoo Records: When you click "Create" buttons, the email content is sent to your Odoo instance to create opportunities, tasks, or tickets
  • Log Emails: When you click "Log Email" buttons, the email content is saved to your Odoo instance's chatter
  • Search and Filter: Search through opportunities, tasks, and tickets associated with contacts
  • Caching: Temporarily cache Odoo API responses locally to improve performance (5-minute TTL)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage APIs. No data is transmitted to any third-party servers except your own Odoo instance.

Data Transmission: The Extension communicates directly with YOUR Odoo instance using HTTPS. Your Odoo credentials and data are never sent to the extension developer or any third party.

Cache Management: Cached data is automatically cleared after 5 minutes or when you click the refresh button. You can also clear all cached data by removing the extension.

Third-Party Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with any third parties.

The Extension only communicates with:

  • Your Odoo Instance: The Extension sends API requests to the Odoo instance URL you configure
  • InboxSDK: A Gmail integration library (https://www.inboxsdk.com/) that helps the Extension interact with Gmail's interface. InboxSDK does not collect or store your email data.

The Extension does NOT:

  • Sell your data to data brokers
  • Share data for advertising purposes
  • Transmit data to analytics services
  • Send data to the extension developer's servers

Google API Services User Data Policy

Odoo Integration for Gmail's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Limited Use Compliance:

  • The Extension only accesses Gmail data when you explicitly open an email
  • Email data is used exclusively for the disclosed purpose: integrating with your Odoo CRM
  • No Gmail data is used for serving advertisements
  • Gmail data is not transferred to third parties (except your own Odoo instance)
  • Gmail data is not used for determining creditworthiness or lending purposes

Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • scripting: Required to inject the sidebar interface into Gmail
  • storage: Required to save your Odoo configuration and cache API responses locally
  • host_permissions (mail.google.com): Required to access Gmail's interface and display the sidebar
  • host_permissions (*.odoo.com): Required to communicate with Odoo cloud instances

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and in your Odoo instance, which you control
  • Deletion: You can clear all Extension data by:
    • Clicking the refresh button (clears cache)
    • Removing the Extension from Chrome (clears all local data)
  • Portability: Your data remains in your Odoo instance and is accessible via Odoo's export features
  • Control: You can revoke the Extension's access at any time by removing it

Data Retention

The Extension retains data as follows:

  • Configuration Data: Stored until you remove the Extension
  • Cached API Responses: Automatically deleted after 5 minutes
  • User Preferences: Stored until you remove the Extension

When you uninstall the Extension, all locally stored data is automatically removed by Chrome.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and, if required, through the Extension's update notifications.

Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
